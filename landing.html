Prayer for Our Nation's Schools

Stand with thousands across America in prayer for protection, revival, and transformation in our schools

Download Prayer

Join Us in This Powerful Prayer

Father, we stand before You today on behalf of the schools of our nation. We repent for allowing You to be removed from our classrooms, hallways, and culture. Forgive us for yielding ground to darkness.

Lord, we grieve the tragic shootings in our schools and the violence that has shaken our nation. We cry out for Your mercy and Your protection over our children. Your Word says, "The Lord is faithful, and He will strengthen you and protect you from the evil one" (2 Thessalonians 3:3). We claim this promise over every student, teacher, and staff member.

We dedicate every school in America‚Äîpublic, private, charter, homeschool, and every college and university‚Äîto You. We consecrate each campus as holy ground where Your presence is welcome and Your purposes prevail. Let every classroom be a refuge of safety, a place of peace, a center of learning, and a wellspring of friendship.

Father, we ask You to station Your angels at the doors and hallways of our schools, to surround our campuses with a hedge of protection (Psalm 91:11). May no weapon formed against our children prosper (Isaiah 54:17). Drive back every spirit of violence, confusion, and fear, and release the light of Christ and the peace of God into every classroom.

We ask for revival in our schools, O Lord. Save our children, heal broken hearts, and let a generation rise up that knows You and lives for You. We pray this in Jesus' mighty name.

Amen.

Our Decree

We decree that God's plumbline of unshakable truth is set again in America's schools.

We declare that classrooms are restored as places of learning and moral strength‚Äîviolence driven out by peace, confusion shattered by clarity, despair overwhelmed by hope. What the enemy meant for evil, God is turning for good.

America's children are taught of the Lord, and great is their peace (Isaiah 54:13).
